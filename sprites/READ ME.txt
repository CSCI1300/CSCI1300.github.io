⚠️ A Quick Note on Animations ⚠️ 

The animation frames included in this sample are **simplified** versions of what's planned for the full paid asset pack.  
The full version will feature smoother animations, and expanded movement sets.
This sample is designed mainly for preview and experimentation.
 

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This sample features the paper doll-style layered system from the full version,
which allows you to build custom characters by combining separate body parts, clothing, and other features.


💻 Who Made This? 💻

I'm CozyFae, a pixel who loves creating cute and cozy sprites for fantasy worlds, farming sims, and RPGs.
I created this pack to help other developers (especially indie devs and solo creators) build characters quickly without starting from scratch.

If you like this sample — the full version will include more bases, animations, and additional styles.
