

FAQ ❓ Frequently Asked Questions – CozyFae Cute Pixel Base – Free Sample

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

What’s different between this sample and the full pack?

This sample includes a **limited number of base parts** and **simplified animation frames**.  
The full version will include:
- More animation frames and smoother motion
- Additional body types or styles (if applicable)
- More clothing, accessories, and customization options

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Can I use these sprites in a game jam?
Absolutely! This sample is perfect for game jams and prototypes.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Can I use these assets for streaming, videos, or tutorials?

Yes, you're welcome to use them in content like game dev streams or tutorials, I would love to see what you make with them —
just be sure to credit me in the description and on-screen.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Do I have to credit you even if I edit the sprites?
Yes. Credit is required whether you use the sprites as-is or modify them. This applies to both personal and commercial projects.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Can I use this sample in a commercial project?
Yes, commercial use is allowed — as long as you **give proper credit** (see LICENSE.txt or ATTRIBUTION.txt for how to do that).

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Can I redistribute or share these assets with others?
No. Please do not re-upload, repackage, or redistribute these assets (even for free).
If someone wants the sample, please direct them to the official itch.io page.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Can I use these assets in NFTs or blockchain projects?
No. These assets may NOT be used in any form of NFT, blockchain, cryptocurrency or any related project — whether commercial or non-commercial.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Can I use these assets for AI training or datasets?
Absolutely not. You are NOT PERMITTED to use these assets to train AI models, 
feed into datasets, or generate derivative content using AI systems.

This includes any form of automated processing that extracts data from the assets, even if modified.


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Can I sell a game that uses this sample?

Yes, you can sell a game that uses the sample assets,
as long as credit is given and the assets are not sold or offered separately as part of your product.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Will the full version ever be free too?
No, the full version is a paid asset pack. This sample is free so you can test it out and see if it fits your needs.
